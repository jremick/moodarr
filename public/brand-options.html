Option 4 kept; five media-inspired marks

Feelerr simple marks

Option 4 remains as the prompt-bubble anchor. The other five now pull from media surfaces: posters, reels, screens, captions, and library stacks.

Option 1

Poster

A single poster tile with an availability dot. It reads as movie/series metadata without becoming a player icon.

Option 2

Reel

A stripped movie-reel silhouette with one request accent. More classic media, still simple at favicon size.

Option 3

Screen

A minimal viewing surface with status. It has the strongest TV-room feel without relying on a play triangle.

Option 4

Prompt

A plain ask bubble for natural-language search. Friendlier, less Arr-coded, and good for the AI companion angle.

Option 5

Captions

A media frame with subtitle lines. It hints at understanding content, not just finding a title.

Option 6

Stack

Two overlapping media cards for local library plus external catalog. It is the most Plex-companion oriented mark.
