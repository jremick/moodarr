Second pass: one silhouette, no product diagram

Feelerr simple marks

These are deliberately stripped down after the first set got too busy. Each option should survive at favicon size before it earns any wordmark or UI treatment.

Option 1

Feeler

One curved sensor stroke with a single tip dot. Best tie to the name and the least generic media-app shape.

Option 2

F Monogram

The safest app icon: a chunky F with one availability dot. It is direct, readable, and hard to confuse.

Option 3

Ping

A presence dot plus one response arc. It says available or found without becoming a radar diagram.

Option 4

Prompt

A plain ask bubble for natural-language search. Friendlier, less Arr-coded, and good for the AI companion angle.

Option 5

Feel Wave

A single mood/signal wave. The most abstract option, but it has a clean shape and avoids video clichés.

Option 6

Notched Dot

A simple disc with a cut-in direction. It reads as selection or fit instead of streaming playback.
