It is normalized from the [Wikidata 2026-06-22 entity dump](https://dumps.wikimedia.org/wikidatawiki/entities/20260622/wikidata-20260622-all.json.bz2). Wikidata makes structured data in its main, property, and lexeme namespaces available under the [Creative Commons CC0 1.0 dedication](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Wikidata:Licensing). The beta asset contains structured text and identifiers, not third-party poster artwork. Its CC0 data status is separate from Moodarr code's Apache License 2.0.

The normalized beta asset contains 90,397 records and is identified by compressed SHA-256 `dd25ba6602e1bdb8e6999b0442bc40165e6d4faadd02e91e74e1a24e2b55e85a`. Of those records, 82,865 have ambiguity-safe local fields for a disclosed Seerr request attempt: 70,841 movies and 12,024 TV series. Thirty-six groups share a strong importer identifier across 72 source records, including 59 that otherwise meet attempt requirements—10 movies and 49 TV series. They remain imported and indexed for provenance and diagnostics, but cannot independently surface in Finder or authorize request actions. An independently identified item that Plex reports as available may remain Finder-visible; catalog ambiguity still blocks every request action. The eligible count is not a claim that Seerr has checked or will accept those titles.
